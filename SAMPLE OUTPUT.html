ME, MYSELF AND I

I am an elementary teacher, but I taught at a secondary level for two years.
I observed that there's a comparison in teaching between the two levels because
you will adjust your method on how to dealing with their differences. Even though
it’s a challenging part for me, I managed it with the proper strategies and
techniques in teaching them. And I also volunteered teacher in my Alma mater for
one year and I taught in kinder and learners with special needs. It’s a tough but
enjoyable experiences because we see in their faces their eagerness to learn and
positive attitudes in doing their task. They open not only my mind but also my heart
their importance in this world. And that’s the reason why I love my profession
because of them.


As a teacher, we compare ourselves to a “Faucet” and our learners to a “Pail”.
It is because we input knowledge to them in academic, non-academic, and most
especially about good morals. That can lead them to become a successful person
in the future.
